The Semantic Web/Cloud Intelligent Systems Management workshop brings together researchers and practitioners to exchange ideas and results on intelligent methods applied to Web- and Cloud-based systems. All accepted papers will be published in the Springer Lecture Notes in Data Engineering and Communication Technologies series and indexed by EI and SCOPUS.

Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Information Retrieval
  • Semantic Grid, Cloud, and Web Methodologies
  • Semantic Grid and Cloud
  • Cloud infrastructures and programming paradigms
  • Service Level Agreements for Cloud services
  • Negotiation, brokering, and management of Cloud resources and services
  • Ontologies
  • Intelligent Agents and Multi-Agent Systems
  • Knowledge Management
  • Semantic Web and Semantic Web Services
  • Business Processes
  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning
  • Generative Artificial Intelligence
  • Process Mining
