University of Luxembourg · July 1–3, 2026
Co-located with CISIS 2026

About the Workshop

The SWISM workshop brings together researchers and practitioners to exchange ideas and results on intelligent methods for Web-, Cloud-, and Edge-based systems. The workshop particularly encourages contributions addressing the convergence of Semantic Web technologies, Cloud–Edge computing, and Artificial Intelligence, with a strong focus on real-world systems and applications.
