Workshop Scope

The Semantic Web/Cloud Intelligent Systems Management workshop brings together researchers and practitioners to exchange ideas and results on intelligent methods applied to Web- and Cloud-based systems. All accepted papers will be published in the Springer Lecture Notes in Data Engineering and Communication Technologies series and indexed by EI and SCOPUS. The workshop encourages contributions addressing the convergence of Semantic Web technologies, Cloud–Edge computing and Artificial Intelligence with a focus on real-world systems and applications.

Topics of Interest

  • Semantic Web and Knowledge Graphs
  • Cloud–Edge–IoT Continuum Architectures
  • AI-Driven Cloud and Service Management
  • Large Language Models for Service Discovery
  • Semantic Interoperability and Data Spaces
  • Service Brokering and Orchestration
  • Autonomous Agents and Multi-Agent Systems
  • Knowledge Management in Cloud Systems
  • Process Mining
  • Service-Level Agreements and QoS
  • Explainable and Trustworthy AI
  • NLP for Cloud Systems
  • Security and Privacy in AI-enabled Clouds
  • Sustainable Cloud and Edge Computing
  • Edge Intelligence
  • Distributed AI Systems
  • Federated Learning
  • Collaborative Edge-Cloud Intelligence
  • AI-Generated Mixed Reality Worlds
  • Industrial Applications
